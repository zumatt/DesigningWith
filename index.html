Designing With: A New Educational Module to Integrate Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Design Curricula, is an ongoing research project in collaboration between the Institute of Design, SUPSI; the EPFL and the Universidade NOVA de Lisboa.

The aim of the project is to develop an experimental approach and collaborate on the definition of a new educational module suitable to be applied in multidisciplinary environments that integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) in Design curricula. This website will provide an online toolkit containing AI/ML tools, resources and methodologies accessible by an audiance of design teachers and students with a traning background in different design areas.

To facilitate learning and exploration of how Artificial Intellingence and Machine Learning could be integrated in design practices, this database organize and visualize AI/ML tools within a novel design system developed by systematizing AI activities within the common stages of design thinking, pursuing the concept of "Learning by searching for".

Design Stage

Research

“Defining and understanding context and target through the analysis of needs, barriers, attitudes and aspirations”

AI Activity

Collect

“Gathering data from physical or digital environments driven by research interests.”

AI Tool

ChatGPT

“ChatGPT is a sibling model to InstructGPT, which is trained to follow an instruction in a prompt and provide a detailed response”
